The concept of this project revolves around the ideea of taking a computer's audio output and generating live captions.


Due to the limited time and resources, our demo aplication captures audio from a microphone and generates live subtitles.


However, with some slight adjutments, the program can be modified to capture audio from different running apps.


Momentarily, the app can only recognize english, but, with greater resources, it is intended to recognize all kinds of different languages.


Our project is meant to help deaf people understand the audio output of their computer for a more complete experience.
